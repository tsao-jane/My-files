The real causes of the indexation gap: (1) primary sitemap excludes blog posts; (2) no inbound links to the blog from anywhere; (3) content published very recently — 47 of 48 posts in the last 8 weeks; (4) high publication velocity on a new subdomain slows Google's crawl appetite.

§ 08

The long view:
subdomain fragmentation.

Beyond the short-term fixes, Neteon's bigger challenge is architectural. Authority is split across eight separately-indexed web properties — each building their own domain reputation in isolation.

Property Platform Purpose
neteon.aiNext.js + VercelProduct showcase + i18n
neteon.ai/blogGhost CMSBlog content (48 posts)
neteon.netWebflowMain B2B site
shopmoxa.neteon.netBigCommerceMoxa e-commerce
ipc.neteon.netBigCommerceIndustrial PCs (Neousys)
iiot.neteon.netBigCommerceIIoT gateways (Teltonika, Planet)
neousys.neteon.net301 redirectDead — redirects to ipc
solutions.neteon.netLegacyOld solutions pages

A blog post on neteon.ai/blog does not pass authority to neteon.net or shopmoxa. Consolidation onto fewer domains — ideally one primary with subdirectories (neteon.net/blog, neteon.net/shop/moxa) — would dramatically improve SEO performance. This is a major architectural decision, not a 90-day fix, but it should be on the roadmap for the next site refresh.
